Answer Questions

Answer each question in the exam. During the exam you will be able to navigate to 'Previous' and 'Next' questions, so you will be able to go back and change your answers should you wish to do so. You may also leave questions unanswered but they will be deemed 'INCORRECT'.

For multiple choice questions tick the checkbox(es) of the answers that you think are correct.

For drag and drop questions you need to click on the possible answer box you think is correct, highlight the text, then drag the text over to whichever box(es) you think it belongs in on the Main Question Panel and drop the text into the appropriate box.
